Description

Inspired by the paradox of a world where technology and nature intertwine, Cyber Flora is a testament to innovation in perfumery. The journey begins with a vibrant burst of Italian Bergamot, digitally enhanced to sparkle with an unprecedented crispness. The heart reveals a bouquet of nocturnal Jasmine and phantom Orchid, cultivated in zero-gravity labs to unlock new facets of their scent. The base grounds the fragrance in a futuristic reality with a blend of smooth, ethically-sourced Sandalwood and a patented molecule, Iso-E Super, that creates a personal, skin-hugging aura of clean, amber-like warmth. It's more than a fragrance; it's an experience of the future, bottled.
